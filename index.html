About the Series

A War Fought in the Shadows
Across All of History

Assassin's Creed follows an ancient conflict between two secret orders — the Assassins, who fight for free will and peace, and the Templars, who seek control and order. Their war has shaped history for thousands of years, hidden in plain sight.

Players relive the memories of legendary assassins through the Animus — a machine that reads genetic memory — traveling across ancient civilizations, empires, and revolutions, uncovering a conspiracy that stretches beyond any single era.
